8-Week Barbell-Only SBD Plan

6 sessions per week focused on Squat, Bench, and Deadlift with exact loads based on your current PRs (115 / 130 / 160 kg). Includes pull-ups, shoulder prehab, and full-body mobility with a built-in deload around week 7.

6×/week · 2 months
Exact weights in kg
Barbell & Pull-ups only

How to Read the Plan

Main lifts use exact kg Accessory barbell work: start light, progress as needed

All Squat / Bench / Deadlift sets are written as sets × reps @ weight in kg. Accessories and pull-ups are given as reps & sets; choose a load you can control and increase slowly when they feel easy.

Training Maxes Used
Bench: 112.5 kg
Squat: 127.5 kg
Deadlift: 156 kg
Structure
3 bench days, 2 squat days, 2 deadlift days (one heavy 3s + one lighter 5s), plus built-in deload in week 7.

Daily Accessories & Mobility Cheatsheet

Shoulder health every day Pull-ups 3x/week minimum
Daily Warm-Up
• 5 min easy cardio
• Dynamic hips/shoulders
• 3–5 ramp-up sets before your first heavy barbell lift
Shoulder Prehab (Most Days)
• Scap push-ups: 2×15
• Band pull-aparts / T-raises: 2×15
• Bar dislocates: 2×10
• Cuban press (empty bar): 2×10
Mobility Focus
After training (5–10 min):
• Deep bodyweight squat hold: 2×30–45 s
• Hamstring hip-hinge stretch: 2×30 s/side
• Ankle knee-over-toe rocks: 2×15/side
• Cat-camel & thoracic rotations
• Extra shoulder dislocates if you feel tight
Accessory Load Guidance
For rows, OHP, lunges, RDLs, etc.:
• Start with a weight you could do 2–3 clean reps more than prescribed.
• When you hit the top of the rep range on all sets for two weeks straight, add 2.5 kg.
